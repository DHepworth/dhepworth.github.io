June 18th, 2023: Being invisible

I used to be a member on many internet-based social media networks: I had an active Facebook presence, I was a regular Twitter user, I posted on Google+ and had an Orkut profile, browsed Reddit daily, shared photos on Instagram, Flickr, and 500px, and posted some videos to YouTube. My contributions to all those communities slowly diminished until I had deleted most of all of them and largely only consumed on the rest.
